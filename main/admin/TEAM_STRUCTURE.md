# Team Structure (4-Folder Split)

Project domain files are organized under `main/` with 4 folders:

- `main/admin/`
	- `src/`
	- `templates/`
	- `static/`

- `main/student/`
	- `src/`
	- `templates/`
	- `static/`

- `main/incharge/`
	- `src/`
	- `templates/`
	- `static/`

- `main/results/`
	- `src/`
	- `templates/`
	- `static/`

Shared/core files remain in root-level/common locations.
